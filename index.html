Why finding Commander seat covers is hard

The Jeep Commander (XK, 2006-2010) has been out of production for over 15 years. Most major brands discontinued Commander-specific fitments years ago. Your best options in 2026 are custom-fit covers sized for the XK platform, or semi-universal SUV covers that work with Commander seat dimensions.

Neoprene or faux leather covers are ideal for aging Commander upholstery — they provide a moisture barrier, UV protection, and the stretch accommodates older seat contours.
